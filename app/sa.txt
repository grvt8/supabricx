Build a Profile & Activity Dashboard for Supabricx with the following specifications:

## 🎨 DESIGN SYSTEM (Match Supabricx existing codebase)

### Colors
- Background: `var(--foreground)` (dark base, #0a0a0a)
- Main Accent: `var(--mainColor)` (orange gradient, replaces green in reference)
- Card/Input Background: `var(--canvas-bg)` (#171717)
- Border: `var(--border-dark)` (#262626)
- Text Primary: `#ffffff`
- Text Secondary: `#a3a3a3`

### Typography
- Headings: Dynapuff (font-display)
- Body/Numbers: DM Mono (font-mono)

### Icons
- Phosphor Icons (@phosphor-icons/react)
